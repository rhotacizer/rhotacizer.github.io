How to use

nunavut

ᓄᓇᕗᑦ

uvannut uqaalaqujunnaqqiuk utiqqat?

ᐅᕙᓐᓄᑦ ᐅᖄᓚᖁᔪᓐᓇᖅᑭᐅᒃ ᐅᑎᖅᑲᑦ?

The -ai series can be typed with e: tememmat ᑌᒣᒻᒪᑦ.

Consonants will attach to the following vowel in case of ambiguity. To push a consonant onto the previous syllable, mark the syllable break with a hyphen (-).

Double L's are converted to the ł-series (ᖤ). They can be split with a hyphen: al-languak ᐊᓪᓚᖑᐊᒃ.
